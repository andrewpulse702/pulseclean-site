Laser Cleaning Technology

Laser cleaning uses high energy pulses of light to remove rust, paint, contaminants and oxidation without damaging the underlying material.

Unlike abrasive blasting or chemical cleaning, laser restoration is precise and non-contact. This allows delicate surfaces such as automotive components and heritage materials to be restored safely.

Laser cleaning is widely used for corrosion removal, surface preparation and restoration projects across multiple industries.
